Frequently Asked Questions

What is an offline modifications generator?
An offline modifications generator is a tool that creates professional documentation for website changes. It generates both internal fulfillment notes for your team and client-facing emails explaining the modifications made to their website. This tool is perfect for web developers, agencies, and freelancers who need to document their work professionally.
How do I use this tool?
Using this tool is simple: (1) Click "Add Page" and enter the webpage URL or page name, (2) Click "Add Change" to describe what modifications you made on that page, (3) Repeat for all pages you've modified, (4) Click the "Generate My Documentation" button. The AI will instantly create both an internal note and a professional client email.
Is this tool free to use?
Yes! This offline modifications generator is completely free to use. No credit card required, no signup needed, and no hidden fees. Generate as many modification notes and emails as you need.
What's the difference between the internal note and client email?
The internal note is formatted for your team's fulfillment documentation with technical details and next steps. The client email is written in friendly, professional language that's easy for clients to understand, focusing on the value and completion of the work.
Can I edit the generated content?
Absolutely! Once generated, you can copy the content and edit it in your preferred text editor or email client. The AI provides a professional starting point that you can customize as needed.
How many pages can I document at once?
You can document as many pages as you need! Simply keep clicking "Add Page" to add more pages to your documentation. The tool will organize all changes by page in both the internal note and client email.
