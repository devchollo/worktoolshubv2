At WorkToolsHub ("we," "us," or "our"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our web application at www.worktoolshub.info ("the Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices outlined below.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses and other personal details provided during registration or authentication.

  • Content Data: Data you input through tools like Note Generation, PDF Combiner, File Uploader, or Knowledge Base, stored securely for Service functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. All data is used internally to improve your WorkToolsHub experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal information and content (e.g., Knowledge Base articles) are stored in a secure MongoDB database and BackBlaze B2, encrypted to protect your data. While we implement reasonable security measures, we are not liable for unauthorized access beyond our control.

We use cookies and local storage to enhance usability, such as saving preferences. You can manage these through your browser settings, but disabling them may impact Service functionality.
